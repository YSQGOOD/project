Security You Can Trust

Astra is built with military-grade encryption, transparent access logs, and global regulatory compliance to keep your identity and data safe — whether you're an individual or an enterprise.

End-to-End Encryption

Your data is encrypted from device to destination—ensuring that only you and authorized parties can access it.

GDPR & FATF Compliance

Astra is built with international standards in mind, adhering to regulations like GDPR and FATF for data protection and financial integrity.

Audit Logs & Permission Trails

Every access, edit, and share is recorded in a tamper-proof log, giving you full visibility and traceability.

Multi-Sig for Sensitive Actions

For high-risk operations, Astra supports multi-signature authentication to add an extra layer of user-controlled security.
